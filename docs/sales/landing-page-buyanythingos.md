Anything. Literally. From a pack of pens to a private jet. For everyday items, she searches the internet instantly. For high-value or complex purchases, she connects you with vetted vendors and brokers through our growing network.

When you request something that requires specialized sourcing — a charter jet, yacht, renovation, rare collectible — Annie sends an RFP to pre-vetted vendors in our network. You get proposals to review, and you choose. No pressure, no bias.

Your conversations and purchase history are private to you. We never sell your data. Annie doesn't share your requirements with vendors until you explicitly approve an RFP.

Google gives you 10 million links. Annie gives you 3 vetted answers with reasoning. No ads, no sponsored results, no affiliate bias. And for luxury purchases, Google can't send an RFP to a yacht broker on your behalf.
